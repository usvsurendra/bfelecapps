Blast Furnace Electrical Apps

A premium Flutter application suite for RINL, Vizag Steel. Manage electrical drawings, motor details, and shift snags with an industrial-grade interface.

RINL, Vizag Steel

Features

Three powerful modules in one unified platform

📐

Drawings App

Browse and access BF Electrical drawings with area-wise categorization. Search across all drawings and open PDFs instantly. Supports BF1, BF2, BF3, BHS, and AUX areas.

⚡

Motor Details

Comprehensive name plate database for blast furnace motors. Search by equipment, make, area, KW, RPM, and frame. Advanced filtering with real-time sync from Google Sheets.

🔧

Shift Snags

Quick reference for PLC and Hardwire troubleshooting. Browse common complaints and solutions organized by category. Search across titles and previews for fast problem resolution.
